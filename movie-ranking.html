The Best Movies According to Aleena

My Top Three Movies

Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara

One of the best Hindi Movie, directed by Zoya Akhtar.
Starring Hrithik Roshan, Farhan Akhtar, Abhay Deol, Katrina Kaif, Kalki Koechilin.
Hundred percent recommended movie for movie night with friends and family.

3 Idiots

An amazing and creative hindi movie about the life of a Teenager and then later on about the adult life.
Starring Aamir Khan, R. Madhavan, Sharman Joshi, Kareena Kapoor, Bomani Irani.
One if the most searched comedy movie on streaming today.

Little Women

A movie portraying the life of 4 sisters having different dreams and problems in their own lives.
Starring Saoirse Ronan, Florence Pugh, Timothee Chalamet, Emma Watson, Eliza Scanien.
